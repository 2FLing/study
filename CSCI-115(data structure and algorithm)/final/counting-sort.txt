counting-sort(A,B,k):
	let C[0..k] to be a new array
	for i = 0 to k:
		C[i] = 0
	for i = 1 to A.len:
		C[A[i]] = C[A[i]] + 1
	for i = 1 to k
		C[i] = C[i] + C[i-1]
	for i = A.len to 1
		B[C[A[i]]] = A[i]
		C[A[i]] = C[A[i]] - 1
				
Time complexity: O(n+k) where k is the range and n is the size of A
